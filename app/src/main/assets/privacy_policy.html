1. Sharing data

We never share your data with anyone.

2. Storing data

KiVi only stores the data it needs to function properly — for as long as you want KiVi to function.

Cloud Service

Ugran is a cloud service. We store messages, photos, videos and documents from your on our servers, so that you can access your data from any of your devices anytime.

3. Deleting data

Accounts

If you would like to delete your account, you can do this by emailing us at support@ugranideas.com . Deleting your account permanently removes all your pass a snap requests, media. This action must be confirmed via your registered email address and cannot be undone.

Messages

Everything you delete is deleted forever.

When you delete a media file, you delete it from your KiVi Gallery. This means that a copy still stays on the server as part of your partner‘s Gallery. As soon as your partner deletes it too, it’s gone forever.

Account self-destruction

We value our disk space greatly. If you stop using KiVi and do not login for at least 6 months, your account will be deleted along with all your pass a snap requests, media and every other piece of data stored in your KiVi account.
